FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

How much does drywall installation cost in Edmonton?

+

Costs vary by scope. Drywall installation and taping typically runs $2–$4 per sq ft. A full project with framing, insulation, drywall and paint can range from $3,000 to $25,000+. Use our estimator or call for a free quote.

Are you WCB insured and licensed in Alberta?

+

Yes. Summit Wall Solutions carries full WCB coverage and holds a valid Edmonton business licence. Documentation available on request.

Do you offer a warranty?

+

Every project is backed by a 1-year workmanship warranty. If anything isn't right, we come back and fix it at no cost.

How long does a project take?

+

A single room is typically 2–4 days; a full basement 1–2 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before any work begins.

Which areas do you serve?

+

Edmonton plus St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, Leduc, Fort Saskatchewan and Stony Plain.

Residential and commercial?

+

Both. Custom homes, basement developments, condo renovations, office fit-outs and commercial interiors.
